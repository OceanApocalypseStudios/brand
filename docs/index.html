Usage Guidance

  • Tide Cyan – Primary interactive elements, CTAs, hero highlights.
  • Abyss Blue – Backgrounds, headers, structural surfaces.
  • Inferno Orange – Emphasis for alerts and key attention points (use sparingly).
  • Foam White – Base backgrounds and negative space.

Reminder: this palette anchors brand recognition. Apps can add functional colors, but keep them harmonious with TrenchRaw.

Do’s, Don’ts & Cautions

Do’s

  • Use the brand palette consistently across marketing and brand materials.
  • Keep typographic hierarchy intact – Display → H1–H4 → Body → Small.
  • Respect logo clear space and proportions.
  • Ensure accessibility and contrast for legibility.
  • Use Material Symbols for icons.

Don’ts

  • Don’t change the official colors for core brand assets.
  • Don’t distort logos or mix typefaces.
  • Don’t overuse Inferno Orange – it’s for emphasis.
  • Don’t place text on low-contrast backgrounds.

Cautions

  • Subtle gradients only; avoid clashing overlays.
  • Don’t go below 12px / 9pt for critical readable copy.
  • Ensure app colors harmonize with the brand palette.
  • Playful tone is allowed but keep clarity and professionalism.
